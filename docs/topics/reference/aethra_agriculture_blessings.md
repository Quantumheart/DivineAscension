# Aethra - Goddess of Light & Agriculture

**Version:** 2.0.0 (Utility Redesign)
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-29
**Status:** Design Phase - Implementation In Progress

---

## Overview

**Domain:** Light, Agriculture, Growth, Warmth
**Philosophy:** *"Light brings life. Tend the land and prosper."*

### Playstyle

Agriculture and cooking focused. Bonuses to crop yields and cooking output. Food satiety for better nutrition.

### Earning Favor

- Harvesting crops: 1 favor per harvest
- Cooking meals: 3-8 favor per meal
- Planting crops: 0.5 favor per plant
- PvP kills: Standard
- Passive: 0.5/hour

---

## Player Blessings (6 Total)

### Tier 1 - Initiate (0-499 favor)
**"Sun's Blessing"**
- **+15%** crop yield
- **+10%** satiety from all food

---

### Tier 2 - Disciple (500-1999 favor) - Choose Path

**Path A: "Bountiful Harvest"** (Agriculture)
- **+20%** crop yield (total: 35%)
- Crops have **20%** chance for bonus seeds

**Path B: "Baker's Touch"** (Cooking)
- Cooking yields **+30%** more food
- Food spoils **20%** slower

*Both require: Sun's Blessing*

---

### Tier 3 - Zealot (2000-4999 favor) - Specialization

**Path A: "Master Farmer"**
- **+25%** crop yield (total: 60%)
- Crops have **30%** chance for bonus seeds (total: 50%)

*Requires: Bountiful Harvest*

**Path B: "Divine Kitchen"**
- Cooking yields **+40%** more (total: 70%)
- Food spoils **30%** slower (total: 50%)

*Requires: Baker's Touch*

---

### Tier 4 - Champion (5000+ favor) - Capstone

**"Avatar of Abundance"**
- **+15%** satiety from all food (total: 25%)
- **+10%** max health

*Requires: Both Master Farmer AND Divine Kitchen*

---

## Religion Blessings (4 Total)

### Tier 1 - Fledgling (0-499 prestige)
**"Community Farm"**

### Tier 2 - Established (500-1999 prestige)
**"Harvest Festival"**
- All members: **+20%** crop yield
- All members: Cooking yields **+20%** more

### Tier 3 - Renowned (2000-4999 prestige)
**"Land of Plenty"**
- All members: **+25%** crop yield
- All members: **+10%** satiety from all food

### Tier 4 - Legendary (5000+ prestige)
**"Pantheon of Light"**
- All members: Food spoils **20%** slower

## Combined Maximums (Player + Religion, Both Paths)

- **85% more crop yield** (60% player + 25% religion)
- **90% more cooking output** (70% player + 20% religion)
- **50% chance for bonus seeds** (50% player path A)
- **50% slower food spoilage** (50% player path B)
- **35% satiety from all food** (25% player + 10% religion)
- **10% max health** (10% player)

---

## Path Choice

**Bountiful Harvest:** Maximize crop production and seed drops
**Divine Kitchen:** Maximize cooking output and food preservation
**Champion:** Requires both paths for satiety and max health
