## Textures

### `assets/divineascension/textures/gui/codex/parchment.png`

- **Source file:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:004_MS_65_PG2_V.jpg
- **Manuscript:** *Très Riches Heures du Duc de Berry*, MS 65, Musée Condé,
  Chantilly (c. 1412–1416, completed c. 1485).
- **Derivation:** A 1200×1200 region of blank vellum cropped from the source
  scan, mirror-tiled and resampled to 512×512 to produce a seamless tile.
- **License:** Public domain — the underlying manuscript is a pre-1928 work
  and the photographic reproduction is a faithful 2D copy of a public-domain
  work (*Bridgeman Art Library v. Corel Corp.*, 36 F. Supp. 2d 191
  (S.D.N.Y. 1999)), which is therefore ineligible for a new copyright in the
  United States.
